The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

Now You See Me

It was the first movie that I've seen where every scene felt like a plot twist was going to happen. There's an impressiveness to that which I'll never get back.

Kung Fu Panda

For a target audience of children, this movie has themes and plots that transcends the children genre.

The Menu

It's so much more than just being a chef or an own of a restaurant. It's an extreme view of someone who had their passion and talent taken advantage of.
